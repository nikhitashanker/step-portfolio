My Projects

Recommender system

The goal of this project was to build a collaborative filtering algorithm that is able to give both book and movie recommendations by predicting user ratings for each of the books and movies they have not rated yet. The algorithm was trained and tested on the BookCrossing and MovieLens public datasets.

SWE Dev Portal

I am currently the technical lead and a developer for the Society of Women Engineers development team at UCLA. We are building a member portal that will allow users to track the events they have attended and register for Evening With Industry, a yearly event.

As a development team member, I:

  • Was responsible for functionality of the Society of Women Engineers club members portal that allows club members to login or logout, build their profile, and register for the SWE event Evening with Industry
  • Gained experience building a REST API, making database changes, and building a basic front-end user interface
